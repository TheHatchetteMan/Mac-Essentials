# Post-Setup Notes

## Manual Steps & Preferences

- Sign into your App Store account and download any paid apps.
- Set up Alfred/Raycast (sign in, customize workflow).
- Restore your VSCode settings or install favorite extensions.
- Sync settings for browsers, sign into Chrome/Firefox.
- Configure Rectangle or Magnet for window management shortcuts.
- Any other app-specific config you care about.

_Add to or update these notes any time your process changes!_
